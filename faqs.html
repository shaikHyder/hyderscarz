Frequently Asked Questions

Can I schedule a test drive?
▼
Yes! You can schedule a test drive directly from the car’s detail page by clicking the "Test Drive" button.
What financing options are available?
▼
We offer flexible EMI plans, zero-down-payment offers, and bank tie-ups with special interest rates.
Are your cars brand new or pre-owned?
▼
We sell both brand-new and certified pre-owned cars with full inspection reports and warranty.
How do I add a car to favorites?
▼
Just click on the ❤️ icon on the car card. All favorites will be saved and accessible in your profile.
